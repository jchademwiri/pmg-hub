# **Playhouse Media Group (PMG) | Comprehensive Business Plan & Brand Structure**

## **1\. Executive Summary**

Playhouse Media Group (PMG) is a multi-division service company providing digital solutions and business compliance services. Operating under a "House of Brands" model, PMG leverages specialized identities to target different sectors of the South African SME market while centralizing management and resources.

* **Vision:** To become a leading service provider in Africa for digital solutions and tender consulting.  
* **Mission:** To help businesses grow by providing professional digital services, compliance support, and tender-winning strategies.  
* **Core Focus:** High-ticket Tendering & Web Development, supported by lead-generating Registration and Graphic services.

## **2\. Brand Architecture (The House of Brands)**

### **Division 1: Web Development (Flagship)**

* **Brand:** [Apex Web Solutions](https://apexwebsolutions.co.za/)  
* **Role:** Recurring income and digital authority.  
* **Services:** Web design, development, hosting, and SEO.

### **Division 2: Tendering Solutions (Primary Revenue Driver)**

* **Recommended Name:** **TenderCore Solutions**  
* **Role:** High-value consulting and primary growth engine.  
* **Services:** Tender compliance, BoQ preparation, pricing, and submissions.

### **Division 3: Company Registrations (Lead Generation Funnel)**

* **Recommended Name:** **BizStart Hub**  
* **Role:** The entry point for new entrepreneurs; feeds all other divisions.  
* **Services:** CIPC, CSD, BEE, COIDA, and CIDB gradings.

### **Division 4: Graphics & Marketing (Support & Upsell)**

* **Recommended Name:** **Playhouse Creative Studio**  
* **Role:** Branding support for web and tender clients.  
* **Services:** Logos, company profiles, and social media assets.

## **3\. Website & Digital Structure**

### **The Corporate Hub: playhousemediagroup.co.za**

The main site acts as the "Portal" for the entire group.

* **Structure:** Home, About Us (Family-Owned Story), Services Overview (4 Cards), Portfolio/Case Studies, and Contact.  
* **Sub-pages:** TenderCore, BizStart, and Creative Studio begin as landing pages under this domain to build SEO authority quickly.

### **The Independent Spoke: apexwebsolutions.co.za**

* Maintains its standalone domain to protect its existing SEO ranking and professional "tech-agency" feel.

## **5\. Workflow & Systems**

### **The "Full Circle" Customer Journey**

1. **Entry (BizStart Hub):** Client registers a new company.  
2. **Immediate Upsell (Creative Studio):** Logo and Company Profile for the new business.  
3. **Intermediate Upsell (Apex Web):** A professional website to establish credibility.  
4. **Growth Upsell (TenderCore):** CIDB grading and preparation for government/private tenders.

### **Tool Stack**

* **CRM/Tracking:** Trello or ClickUp (One board per division).  
* **Storage:** Google Drive (Secure cloud storage for ID docs and SARS files).  
* **Communication:** Shared inbox (info@playhousemediagroup.co.za).

## **7\. Strategic Advisory & Recommendations**

### **A. Revenue Prioritization (The 60/30/10 Rule)**

Do not treat all divisions as equal. To maximize growth:

* **60% Focus:** **TenderCore Solutions** (High-ticket deals).  
* **30% Focus:** **Apex Web Solutions** (Steady recurring income).  
* **10% Focus:** **BizStart/Creative** (Lead generation & support).

### **B. Positioning & Messaging**

Position PMG as the **"Growth Partner for Contractors and SMEs."** This allows you to speak directly to construction firms who need CIDB, CSD, a website, and tender help all at once.

### **C. The "Business-in-a-Box" Bundle**

Create a high-conversion package for new founders:

* **Package:** CIPC \+ BEE \+ Logo \+ 1-Page Website.  
* **Goal:** Secure the client early so they come to you for their first tender.

### **D. Portfolio Strategy**

Proof is everything in Tendering and Web Dev.

* Document every tender worked on (successes and lessons).  
* Showcase BoQs and compliance files (redacting sensitive info) to prove technical competence.

### **E. Risk Management**

* **POPIA Compliance:** Never share client IDs over insecure channels.  
* **SLAs:** Ensure web clients sign a maintenance agreement so you aren't liable for expired domains.  
* **Cash Flow:** Always request upfront payments for registrations and milestones for web/tender projects.
