## Who We Are

Tender Edge Solutions (TES) is a specialist tender compliance and bid preparation consultancy serving South African businesses in construction, security, cleaning, and the broader SME sector.

We help businesses become fully compliant and competitively positioned to win government and private-sector contracts — from first-time tender applicants to established contractors looking for a reliable compliance partner.

TES is proudly South African, operating within the Gauteng region with a growing national footprint.

> *"We don't just prepare paperwork — we prepare businesses to win."*

## The Problem We Solve

Every year, South African SMEs lose government tenders not because they can't do the work — but because their compliance documents are outdated, incomplete, or incorrectly formatted.

**Tender Edge Solutions fixes that.**

Whether you're new to tendering, have failed a bid on a technicality, or need a trusted partner to keep your compliance calendar running — TES is your edge.

## How We Work

Our process is simple, structured, and results-oriented:

1. **Free Tender Readiness Check** — Submit your company details. We review your CSD, CIDB, BEE, and COIDA status and identify any compliance gaps.
2. **Custom Compliance Plan** — We present a short compliance report and recommend the right service package for your needs.
3. **Document Preparation & Submission** — We handle everything: registrations, formatting, and compilation.
4. **Ongoing Support** — Stay compliant year-round with our monthly retainer.
