## What We Do

Apex Web Solutions designs and develops custom websites for South African businesses that want a strong, professional online presence. We handle everything from design and development to hosting, domain registration, email setup, and ongoing maintenance.

Our clients range from new entrepreneurs needing their first website to contractors and government suppliers who need a credible online presence to support their tender applications.
