# Tender Edge Solutions (TES)
### Brand Profile — Division 01 of Playhouse Media Group

## Who We Are

Tender Edge Solutions is the tender compliance and bid preparation division of Playhouse Media Group. We help South African businesses — particularly contractors and SMEs in construction, security, and cleaning — become fully compliant and competitive in the government and private-sector tender market.

TES is the **primary revenue driver** of PMG, representing 60% of total business focus. In 2025, Jacob served approximately 5 tender clients informally — 1 requiring full ongoing tender preparation, and 4 requiring compliance support for specific tenders. This existing client base is the foundation to scale from.

> **Note on naming:** TenderTrack 360 (tendertrack360.co.za) is a separate SaaS product — a software platform for tracking and managing tenders. Tender Edge Solutions is the human consultancy service.

## What We Do

Tender Edge Solutions prepares South African businesses to win government and private-sector contracts. From first compliance registration to a fully packaged tender submission, we provide end-to-end support across the entire tender readiness journey.

We specialise in businesses that are new to tendering, have lost tenders due to compliance gaps, or need a reliable ongoing partner to keep their compliance current so they can bid with confidence every time.
