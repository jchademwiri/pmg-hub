# 9. STRATEGIC ADVISORY SUMMARY

*(Synthesised from PMG founders, Claude AI, Gemini, and ChatGPT inputs)*

1. **Revenue Focus** - 60% TenderCore, 30% Apex, 10% everything else. Do not treat all divisions equally.
2. **Portfolio First** - Proof of work is more valuable than any ad. Document every tender, every website, every win.
3. **Systems Before Scaling** - Build proposal templates, file systems, and CRM tracking before spending on ads.
4. **Offer Bundling** - Business-in-a-Box and Tender-Ready bundles increase deal size and lock in multi-division clients.
