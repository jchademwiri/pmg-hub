# 7. Items And Daily Billing Workflow

> Items make quotes and invoices faster and more consistent.

## What Items Are

Items are reusable services or products, such as:

- Website design
- Monthly SEO
- Tender document review
- Graphic design per hour

Use items so descriptions, prices, and reporting stay consistent.

## Daily Workflow

| Situation | What To Do |
|-----------|------------|
| New client asks for price | Create a quote |
| Client accepts | Create or convert to invoice |
| Work is ready to bill | Issue invoice |
| Money arrives | Record payment |
| Client was overcharged | Create credit note |
| Client asks what they owe | Open account or statement |

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Quote accepted, now what? | Create an invoice |
| Invoice has wrong amount | Void and recreate if issued, or edit if still draft |
| Client paid but invoice is not paid | Record and allocate the payment |
| Wrong division appears | Check the document division |
| Statement looks wrong | Check invoice, payment, and credit allocations |
