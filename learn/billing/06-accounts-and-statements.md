# 6. Billing Accounts And Statements

> Client accounts show what each client has been billed, paid, and still owes.

## Billing Accounts

A client billing account tracks:

- Invoices
- Payments
- Credit notes
- Outstanding balance
- Payment history

Use this when someone asks, "What does this client owe?"

## Statements

A statement is a period summary for a client.

It usually shows:

- Opening balance
- New invoices
- Payments received
- Credit notes
- Closing balance

## When To Send A Statement

Send a statement when:

- A client asks for their balance
- You are following up overdue invoices
- A client paid multiple invoices and needs a summary
- You want a clean record of account activity

## Ageing

Ageing shows how old unpaid amounts are.

| Bucket | Meaning | Risk |
|--------|---------|------|
| Current | Not overdue or less than 30 days | Low |
| 30 Days | 31-60 days overdue | Medium |
| 60 Days | 61-90 days overdue | High |
| 90 Days | More than 90 days overdue | Urgent |
