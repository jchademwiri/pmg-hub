# 3. Billing Settings

> Billing settings control document and email setup for each division.

## Common Divisions

| Acronym | Division |
|---------|----------|
| AWS | Apex Web Solutions |
| TES | Tender Edge Solutions |
| PMG | Playhouse Media Group |

## What To Configure

For each division, check:

- Billing contact details
- Banking details
- Invoice defaults
- Quote defaults
- Email sender details
- Notes and terms
- Logo or document identity

## Why Billing Settings Matter

They affect:

- Quotes
- Invoices
- Statements
- Payment receipts
- Email delivery
- PDF output

## Common Problems

| Problem | What To Check |
|---------|---------------|
| Wrong logo on document | Division billing setup and organisation logo |
| Missing bank details | Billing settings for that division |
| Email looks incomplete | Sender/contact settings |
| Wrong division identity | Source quote or invoice division |
