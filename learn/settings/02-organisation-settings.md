# 2. Organisation Settings

> Organisation settings control the business identity shown across the app.

## What To Check

Confirm these details are correct:

- Business name
- Contact email
- Phone number
- Address
- Logo

## Why This Matters

Organisation details can appear in:

- Document previews
- Generated PDFs
- Emails
- Admin pages
- Reports

If something looks wrong on a quote, invoice, or PDF, check Organisation and Billing settings first.

## Safe Editing Checklist

1. Confirm the correct business details.
2. Upload or verify the logo.
3. Check the address.
4. Save changes.
5. Generate or preview a document to confirm it looks right.
