# 3. Reports And Drilldowns

> Reports explain the numbers. Drilldowns show the transactions behind them.

## Common Reports

| Report | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| Revenue | Income by client, division, and month |
| Expenses | Costs by category and division |
| Profit & Loss | Revenue minus expenses and allocations |
| Division | Performance by `AWS`, `TES`, and `PMG` |

## How To Read Reports

1. Choose the period.
2. Check the summary first.
3. Look for unusual movement.
4. Drill into large or surprising numbers.
5. Compare with the previous month.

## Drilldowns

When a number is clickable, open it to see the transactions behind the total.

Use drilldowns when:

- A total looks too high
- A total looks too low
- A category changed sharply
- A division result needs explanation

## Teaching Tip

Do not guess from memory. Use the report and drilldown numbers as the source of truth.
