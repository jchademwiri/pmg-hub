# 2. Snapshots

> A snapshot is a locked monthly financial record.

## What A Snapshot Means

Once a month is closed:

- The month becomes a permanent record
- The numbers do not change later
- Users can compare months confidently
- The dashboard badge shows the actual closed period

Example: if you are in June, June is still open. The system may show **May 2026 closed** because May is the completed month that was locked.

## What The Snapshot Stores

- Revenue
- Expenses
- PMG Share
- Profit/Loss
- Notes, if entered during close

## How To Close A Month

1. Review income and expenses.
2. Check draft invoices and incomplete items.
3. Click the dashboard close button for the completed period, for example **Close May 2026**.
4. Review the figures.
5. Confirm the close.
6. Review the result in Insights -> Snapshots.

## What To Look For

| Question | Look At |
|----------|---------|
| Which months are closed? | Snapshot list |
| How much did we make? | Revenue and Profit/Loss |
| What did we spend? | Expenses |
| Can this month still change? | If it is not closed, yes |
