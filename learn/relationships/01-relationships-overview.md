# 1. Relationships Overview

> Relationships is the system's address book and sales pipeline.

## What Relationships Is For

Use Relationships to manage:

- Clients
- Leads
- Divisions

Relationships connects directly to Billing and Finance.

```text
Lead -> Client -> Quote -> Invoice -> Payment -> Reports
```

## What New Users Should Remember

- Create or confirm the client before billing.
- Use leads for people or businesses that have not become clients yet.
- Choose the correct division when creating client, quote, invoice, income, or expense records.
- Do not delete relationship history just because a lead goes cold or a client becomes inactive.

## Quick Reference

| I want to... | Go to... |
|-------------|----------|
| View clients | Relationships -> Clients |
| Add a potential client | Relationships -> Leads |
| Track pipeline stage | Relationships -> Leads |
| Check business unit ownership | Relationships -> Divisions |
