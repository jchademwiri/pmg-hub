# 2. Clients

> A client is a person or business you work with.

## What A Client Record Shows

A client record can include:

- Name and contact details
- Assigned division
- Quotes
- Invoices
- Payments
- Outstanding balance
- Activity history

## Client Financial Health

| Metric | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Total Invoiced | How much has been billed |
| Total Paid | How much has been received |
| Outstanding Balance | What is still owed |
| Last Activity | Most recent client-related activity |

## Basic Workflow

1. Search for the client before creating a duplicate.
2. Add the client if they do not exist.
3. Confirm contact details.
4. Confirm division.
5. Use Billing to create quotes or invoices.
6. Use the client view to review history.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Duplicate client created | Ask an admin before changing history |
| Wrong division on a document | Check the quote or invoice division |
| Balance looks wrong | Review invoices, payments, and credit notes |
