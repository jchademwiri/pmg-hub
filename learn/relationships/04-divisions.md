# 4. Divisions

> Divisions are the business units used across relationships, billing, finance, and reports.

## Common Divisions

| Acronym | Division | Typical Use |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| AWS | Apex Web Solutions | Web, digital, and technical services |
| TES | Tender Edge Solutions | Tender support services |
| PMG | Playhouse Media Group | Core group and media services |

## Why Divisions Matter

Divisions affect:

- Quote and invoice numbering
- Document logos
- Revenue reporting
- Expense reporting
- Distribution rules
- Performance by business unit

## Where You See Divisions

| Area | How Division Is Used |
|------|----------------------|
| Clients | Shows ownership or primary relationship |
| Quotes | Sets document identity |
| Invoices | Sets document identity and reporting |
| Payments | Follows invoice and income context |
| Expenses | Shows which division spent money |
| Reports | Compares business unit performance |

## Rule Of Thumb

If a document or transaction belongs to a specific business unit, choose that division at the source. Reports follow the source record.
