# 1. Finance Overview

> Finance shows money coming in, money going out, and how the business is performing.

## What Finance Is For

Use Finance to:

- Review income
- Record expenses
- Manage expense categories
- View distributions
- Understand division performance

## The Main Flow

```text
Billing payments -> Finance income
Expenses         -> Finance expenses
Finance          -> Accounting
Accounting       -> Reports
```

## What New Users Should Remember

- Invoice payments should usually start in Billing -> Payments.
- Non-invoice income can be recorded in Finance -> Income.
- Expenses are recorded in Finance -> Expenses.
- Categories matter because they control how expenses appear in reports.
- Divisions matter because they show which business unit earned or spent the money.
